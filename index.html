Policy

Our Booking Policy

Thank you for choosing Glamour Studio! Your support means the world to us. Please review our policy below to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience.

  • A non-refundable R100 deposit is required to secure an appointment.
  • No appointment will be considered without a booking fee.
  • Proof of payment must be sent to confirm a booking (no screenshots).
  • Please let us know when booking if you need a soak-off.
  • The remaining service fee/balance is paid after the set is complete.
  • Do not be late. Being 15 minutes late will result in cancellation and forfeiture of the deposit.
  • Reschedule 24 hours before your appointment to avoid losing your booking fee.
  • Rescheduling is only allowed once.
